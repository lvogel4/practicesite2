Lauren Vogel



Introductions



Hello, everyone! My name is Lauren Vogel. I am twenty-seven, about to turn twenty-eight in July, and am a student at George Mason University. I am currently working towards my English degree and hope one day to become a published author.

Interests and degrees



My current passions include fanfiction, specifically Star Wars although I occasionally branch out into other fandoms. I have been practicing fanfiction since I was eleven years old when my eye doctor, of all people, recommended it to me thanks to his daughter being into fanfiction. (Thank you, Doctor!)
I believe that fanfiction is a valid form of storytelling, and can even dodge around issues that original fiction unfortunately can get hobbled by, such as the executives meddling in things.
My degree I'm aspiring to is a Bachelor's Degree. Avenue Q might have asked once what you do with a B.A. in English, but I'll find a way.
